Plant Care Guide

  1. Watering:
    • Ensure proper watering based on the plant's needs.
    • Check the soil moisture regularly.
    • Use room-temperature water.
    • Adjust watering frequency according to seasonal changes.
  2. Lighting:
    • Place indoor plants in suitable locations with appropriate lighting conditions.
    • Monitor sunlight exposure.
    • Some plants may require specific lighting conditions.
  3. Temperature and Humidity:
    • Maintain consistent room temperatures.
    • Provide adequate humidity for tropical plants.
  4. Soil and Fertilizing:
    • Use well-draining soil mixes.
    • Fertilize plants during their active growing season.
  5. Pruning and Maintenance:
    • Trim dead or yellowing leaves.
    • Prune overgrown stems or branches.
    • Regularly inspect plants for signs of pests or diseases.
  6. Repotting:
    • Repot plants as needed.
    • Refresh the potting mix every one to two years.
  7. Seasonal Care:
    • Adjust care routines seasonally.
    • During the growing season, plants may require more frequent watering and feeding.
    • In winter, reduce watering frequency.
